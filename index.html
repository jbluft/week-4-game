Tribbles Collector

Tribbles have long been known as the scourge of the galaxy. Collect them and rid us all of their incessant cooing.

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four tribbles below. By clicking on a tribble you will add amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number; you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each tribble is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each tribble.
